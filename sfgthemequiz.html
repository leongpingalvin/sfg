T H E M E    —    O N L I N E    Q U I Z

Consider the following text:

Alexander Popov stole the show at the European swimming championships in Helsinki on Tuesday, producing a sizzling 100m freestyle time which nobody but he himself has ever matched. The Russian Olympic champion swam a languid heat in the morning and then burst out with an astonishing semi-final swim of 48.34sec.

1. How many marked themes are there in the text?

There aren't any
There aren't any, because of ellipsis
One—"producing"
Two—"Alexander Popov" and "producing"

2. What is the topical theme of "and then burst out with an astonishing semi-final swim of 48.34sec"?

"and"
"then"
"he" as the ellipsed topical theme
There isn't any

3. What can you say about the themes in the text?

They are predicated
Some are unmarked and some are marked
All are marked
All are unmarked

4. Is "then" in the final clause of the text thematic?

No, because the topical theme has been ellipsed
No, because "then" is not a topical theme
Yes, because it is situated near the beginning of the clause
Uncertain, because the position of the ellipsed topical theme is uncertain

5. Identify all the non-topical themes in the text.

"on Tuesday"
"and"
"and", "but"
There aren't any

6. Which of the following (re-written) ranking clauses contains a textual theme?

"The show, Alexander Popov stole at the European swimming championships in Helsinki on Tuesday"

7. Which of the following (re-written) clauses contains an interpersonal theme?
