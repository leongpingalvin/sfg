Consider the following text:

Alexander Popov stole the show at the European swimming championships in Helsinki on Tuesday, producing a sizzling 100m freestyle time which nobody but he himself has ever matched. The Russian Olympic champion swam a languid heat in the morning and then burst out with an astonishing semi-final swim of 48.34sec.

1. Which one of the following represents a relational process?

"stole"
"producing"
"burst out"
None of the above

Mental
Material
Circumstantial

5. Provide an analysis of the following clause: "The Russian Olympic champion swam a languid heat in the morning"

"The Russian Olympic champion" (Participant: Actor)
          "swam" (Process: Behavioural)
          "a languid heat" (Participant: Behaviour)
          "in the morning" (Circumstance: Time)
"The Russian Olympic champion" (Participant: Actor)
          "swam" (Process: Material)
          "a languid heat" (Participant: Scope)
          "in the morning" (Circumstance: Time)
"The Russian Olympic champion" (Participant: Actor)
          "swam" (Process: Material)
          "a languid heat" (Participant: Goal)
          "in the morning" (Circumstance: Time)
"The Russian Olympic champion" (Participant: Goal)
          "swam" (Process: Material)
          "a languid heat" (Participant: Scope)
          "in the morning" (Circumstance: Time)
